My new Chromebook

Santa got me a Chromebook last Christmas (maybe it was myself, I don't know any more). Now my goal is to make cloud development. The whole 9 yards. With a cloud IDE, cloud servers and all that jazz. And here I write down what I have learned.

The cloud IDE

To be frank my decision making process for the cloud IDE was quite simple.
I tried two them ( CloudEnvy and Cloud9), found Cloud9 the better solution and chose it.
Why do I like it more?
  1. You have full Linux VM with all a geek could dream of (Git, Python, Perl, ...)
  2. That's all and more than enough. All the other goodies (like a connection to Github) does Codenvy have too.
